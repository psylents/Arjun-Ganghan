SEO

The practice of improving a website's or web page's visibility in search engine results pages is called search engine optimization. SEO may dramatically increase a website's organic traffic and increase its visibility and accessibility to the target audience by strategically optimizing it for relevant keywords.

SEM

The goal of SEM, or search engine marketing, is to increase a website's visibility in search engine results. Paid advertising—such as Google Ads—and optimization techniques—such as SEO—are combined to accomplish this.

The strategic use of various social media channels to promote goods and services is known as social media marketing (SMM). By effectively utilizing these platforms, companies may quickly reach a large audience, increasing brand awareness and engagement.
